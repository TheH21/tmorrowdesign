ABOUT ME

A JACK OF ALL TRADES, A MASTER OF SOME

I have spent my life developing skills in a variety of trades, some of which I have managed to form into a career, all of which benefit from my endless interest in design in every aspect of the term. Nothing compares to the experience of watching an idea grow from a rough concept in my mind to a tangible product in the hands of my clients, which is something that my business partner Matt Hallman and I have the opportunity to witness frequently in the custom pieces of furniture we craft through our company, Hallman Woodworks. Born and raised in Roseburg, Oregon, I will never stop working with my hands or enjoying the wonders the Pacific Northwest has to offer.
